About Me

Welcome! I am Merat Semma. and I like to share more about my journey as a Molecular Biophysics and Biochemistry student at Yale University, my passion for biochemistry, and my commitment to global health initiatives.

I am deeply engaged in research aimed at understanding how RNA impacts human health, and I have a specific focus on discovering new ways to address health challenges in underserved communities. Through my academic work and global health efforts, I strive to make a meaningful impact on women's and children's health.

When I’m not in the lab, I’m often involved in leadership activities, helping to mentor and guide younger students while advocating for diversity and inclusivity in the scientific community. I’m excited to continue exploring opportunities that allow me to merge my love for science and service.
